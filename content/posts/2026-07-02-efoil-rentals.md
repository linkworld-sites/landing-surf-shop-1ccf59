## What You Don't Need to Bring

Swimwear and a towel. That's genuinely it.

We have wetsuits available if the water temperature calls for it. Sunscreen is smart but not required. Leave the fins at home — we're not surfing today.

## How Long Are the Sessions, Really?

When we say 45 minutes, we mean 45 minutes of *water time*. The pre-session safety brief and gear fit happen before the clock starts. You're not paying for us to explain the remote.

Arrive 15 minutes before your slot and you'll be in the water on time, every time.

## Good to Know Before You Book

**Can beginners rent without a lesson?** Yes, if you've ridden an eFoil before — or any kind of foil board. If this is your very first time, we'd strongly recommend a lesson first. You'll learn faster, you'll be safer, and honestly you'll have more fun on your rentals afterward. Check out [our beginner lessons](/blog/2026-07-02-efoil-lessons-beginners).

**Minimum age:** 16 with a guardian present; 18 to ride independently.

**Weight limit:** Our boards comfortably handle up to 120kg / 265lbs.

**Weather cancellations:** If we cancel due to conditions, you get a full refund or a rescheduled slot — your choice.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Do I need to know how to surf to rent an eFoil?</h3>

No surf background needed. eFoiling is its own skill set — the board is motorized and doesn't require wave timing. Riders who've never surfed pick it up just as fast as experienced surfers (sometimes faster, since they have fewer bad habits to unlearn).
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How fast do eFoils go?</h3>

Our rental boards are governed to a safe recreational speed — roughly 25–30 km/h depending on conditions. Fast enough to feel genuinely airborne. Not so fast that it gets sketchy. If you've unlocked your technique and want to push further, ask about our advanced sessions.
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>What if I've never foiled but want to rent instead of taking a lesson?</h3>

Brave move. We won't stop you — but we will give you an honest heads-up: first-timers who rent cold spend a lot of their session getting back on the board. That's still fun, but a 30-minute intro lesson means you spend 80% of your rental actually flying instead of swimming next to the board. Your call.
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Can I ride with a friend at the same time?</h3>

eFoils are single-rider only — the board is designed for one person. But you can book back-to-back or parallel slots so you're both on the water at the same time. Groups of 4+ get a discount; hit us up when you book.
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>What happens if I crash?</h3>

You get wet. Crashing is part of learning, and the equipment is built for it — these boards take a beating and come back fine. Our staff is always nearby. The impact vest handles the rest.
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How far in advance should I book?</h3>

Weekend slots go fast — we'd recommend booking at least a week out in peak season. Weekday afternoons are more flexible. Walk-ins are occasionally possible but never guaranteed.
</div>
